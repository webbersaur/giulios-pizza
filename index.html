A Family Tradition Since 1987

Giulio's Pizza Restaurant was founded by the Gagliardi family with a simple mission: to bring the authentic flavors of Italy to North Haven, Connecticut.

All of our chefs - Giulio, Michelina, and Salvatore - were born in Italy and bring generations of culinary tradition to every dish we prepare. Our pizza dough is made fresh daily, and we use only the finest ingredients.
