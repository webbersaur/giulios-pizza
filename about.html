Our Story

A family tradition of authentic Italian cuisine

Family-Owned Since 1987

Giulio's Pizza Restaurant has been a cornerstone of the North Haven community for over 35 years. What started as a dream of bringing authentic Italian flavors to Connecticut has grown into a beloved family gathering place for generations of customers.

Founded by the Gagliardi family, our restaurant was built on the traditions passed down through generations in Italy. Every recipe, every technique, and every dish reflects our deep roots in Italian culinary heritage.

When you walk through our doors, you become part of our extended family. We take pride in knowing our regular customers by name and welcoming new faces with the same warmth and hospitality that has defined Italian trattorias for centuries.

What Sets Us Apart

Our commitment to quality and tradition

Fresh Dough Daily

Every morning, we prepare our pizza dough from scratch using our traditional family recipe. No shortcuts, no compromises - just pure, authentic flavor in every bite.

Authentic Recipes

Our recipes have been passed down through generations of Italian cooks. We honor those traditions while serving the North Haven community with pride.

Quality Ingredients

We source the finest ingredients for every dish. From our imported Italian cheeses to our locally-grown vegetables, quality is never compromised.
