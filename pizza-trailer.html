Mobile Wood-Fired Excellence

Experience the same authentic Italian flavors you love at Giulio's Pizza Restaurant, now available for your special events! Our wood-fired pizza trailer brings the tradition and taste of our family recipes directly to you.

Our portable wood-burning oven reaches temperatures over 800°F, creating pizzas with that perfect crispy crust and smoky flavor that only true wood-fired cooking can achieve. Each pizza is handcrafted with fresh dough made daily and topped with premium ingredients.

From intimate backyard gatherings to large corporate events, we bring the full Giulio's experience to your location.

The Wood-Fired Difference

What makes our trailer special

Authentic Wood-Fired Oven

Our portable oven reaches over 800°F, cooking pizzas in just 90 seconds with that perfect char and smoky flavor you can only get from real wood fire.

Fresh Ingredients

We bring the same quality ingredients from our restaurant - fresh dough made daily, premium mozzarella, and our signature sauce.

Live Entertainment

Watch our skilled pizzaiolos hand-toss dough and craft each pizza right before your eyes. It's dinner and a show!
